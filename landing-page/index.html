About Kram

Building the future of education management

Empowering Indian institutions with technology that understands their needs.

Our Story

Kram (meaning "sequence" or "order" in Sanskrit) was born from a simple observation: education management is broken. Institutions run on fragmented tools, scattered spreadsheets, and disconnected workflows—leading to chaos instead of clarity. From tracking attendance and managing fees to organizing semesters and generating reports, nothing talks to each other.

We built Kram to bring order to that chaos. A unified platform that connects every workflow, surfaces real-time insights, and simplifies every aspect of institutional management—from admissions to graduation.

Our Mission

To empower every educational institution in India with modern, efficient, and affordable technology that transforms the learning experience.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted education management platform in India, enabling institutions to focus on what matters most—education.

Our Values

Innovation, simplicity, reliability, and a deep commitment to understanding the unique needs of Indian education.
