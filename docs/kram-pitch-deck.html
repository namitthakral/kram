01

Kram

The operating system for parent-school engagement

Improving student outcomes through structured communication, real-time insights, and actionable guidance.

02

🚨 Problem

Parents lack visibility into:

  • What their child is learning
  • Where they are struggling

Communication is fragmented:

  • WhatsApp groups = noise, not clarity
  • No structured updates
  • Teachers spend time manually updating parents

Schools rely on:

  • Occasional PTMs (once every few months)
  • Static report cards
Result: Low engagement, delayed intervention, poor student outcomes
03

💡 Solution

Kram connects parents, teachers, and students on one platform

  • Real-time visibility into student progress
  • Structured communication (no chaos)
  • Actionable insights for parents

Core pillars:

📊
Progress tracking
💬
Communication
🎯
Action-driven improvement
04

📱 Product

A simple, daily-use app for parents and schools

  • Home dashboard: Daily snapshot of child's progress
  • Actions tab: What parents should do today
  • Progress tab: Academic + behavioral insights
  • Weekly reports: Clear, structured summaries
  • PTM integration: Better, data-driven conversations
Designed for simplicity and consistency of use
05

🔥 Why Now

Macro tailwinds are aligned

  • Smartphone penetration among parents is at an all-time high
  • Schools are digitizing post-COVID
  • Parents are more involved in their child's education
  • Shift from "learning apps" → "outcome-driven tools"
The infrastructure exists. The layer for engagement is missing.
06

🎯 Key Features

Built to drive real behavior change

  • Weekly Parent Reports → Clear insights, not just data
  • Action-based recommendations → "What to do next" for parents
  • Notification + habit loop → Drives daily engagement
  • PTM tracking & preparation → Makes meetings more effective
